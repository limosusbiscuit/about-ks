An energetic city filled with spirit and and pride. Wichita is located at the heart of Kansas and is a very popular central area for people traveling across the state. The city of Wichita is considered Urban.

Cool Facts

  • Wichita is known as the "Air Capital of the World" due to its contributions to the aviation industry.
  • The city features the iconic Keeper of the Plains statue, located at the middle of the Arkansas River.
  • Wichita is home to the largest and longest-running community theater in the U.S., the Wichita Community Theatre.
