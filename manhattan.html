A charming and vibrant college town known as "The Little Apple," where scenery meets beautiful landscape, and home to Kansas State University. Welcome to Manhattan! Located in northeastern Kansas, this rural town doesn't feel so rural during college season.

Cool Facts

  • Manhattan is nicknamed "The Little Apple," a nod to New York City's Manhattan.
  • It is home to Kansas State University, a major research and educational institution influencing the town's lively atmosphere.
  • The city hosts one of the largest country music festivals in the Midwest, the Country Stampede
