Welcome to the heart of the Midwest! Known for its sweeping prairies, rich agricultural, and a history deeply rooted in the American story, Kansas is a state that captivates with its simplicity and surprises with its hidden gems. From the streets of Wichita to the serene Flint Hills, Kansas offers a blend of urban charm and natural beauty.

Cool Facts

  1. Kansas has some of the highest average wind speeds in the country!
  2. Kansas is the home of Dorthy from "The Wizard of Oz"
  3. Wichita, Kansas, is known as the "Air Capital of the World" due to its major role in aircraft manufacturing.
