Topeka! the heart of Kansas and a city rich in history and vibrant culture! Settled along the banks of the Kansas River, Topeka is not just the state capital but a place where the warmth of Midwestern hospitality meets a bustling hub of history, art, and natural beauty. Topeka is located in the Northwest side of Kansas right near the boarder. Topeka is classified as an urban community.

Cool Facts

  • Topeka played a major role in American history as the birthplace of the landmark 1954 Brown v. Board of Education Supreme Court case!
  • The name "Topeka" is derived from a Native American language, meaning "a good place to dig potatoes."
  • The Kansas State Capitol is one of the tallest domes in the U.S., standing at 304 feet!
